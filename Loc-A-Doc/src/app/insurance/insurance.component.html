Do you have insurance?

In the United States of America, many clinics and hospitals require that patients have health insurance before agreeing to treat them. Health insurance is a type of insurance that pays for part or all of your care when you are treated by healthcare professionals. Having insurance increases the amount of options you have when looking for healthcare providers.
